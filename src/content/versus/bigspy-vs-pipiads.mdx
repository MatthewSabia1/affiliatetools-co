## Where BigSpy wins

**Six-platform coverage in one seat.** Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, Pinterest. PiPiADS is effectively TikTok-only. For researchers who need Meta or YouTube visibility alongside TikTok, BigSpy is the only option between these two — and you don't pay for a separate tool for each surface.

**Lowest entry cost.** BigSpy Basic at $9/mo and a free tier on top. PiPiADS Starter is $77/mo. That's an 8x gap at the entry level. For operators auditing the category or scaling slowly, BigSpy is the approachable first step.

**Better for generalist brand research.** If your question is "what are brands running across the web right now?", BigSpy's cross-platform index answers that. PiPiADS answers a narrower question (TikTok specifically) and doesn't help with cross-platform competitive scans.

**Meta coverage included.** BigSpy indexes Facebook and Instagram alongside TikTok. PiPiADS doesn't meaningfully cover Meta. For operators who split media across Meta and TikTok, BigSpy consolidates the research.

**Pinterest and Twitter.** Niche but real. Pinterest matters for e-commerce and lifestyle verticals. Twitter matters for SaaS and B2B ad research. Neither is in PiPiADS's scope. BigSpy includes both.

**Faster onboarding for generalists.** BigSpy's neutral filter-and-browse UI is usable within an hour. PiPiADS assumes familiarity with TikTok-specific metadata — creator handles, hashtag conventions, TikTok Shop taxonomies. For researchers without TikTok fluency, BigSpy is the easier first tool.

**Meta visibility in the same seat.** Facebook and Instagram remain the largest paid-social platforms by revenue. PiPiADS's TikTok-only scope ignores this, forcing a second tool buy for Meta coverage. BigSpy includes Meta alongside TikTok — one seat handles the two biggest surfaces simultaneously.

## Where PiPiADS wins

**Deepest TikTok Ads Library in the category.** PiPiADS is purpose-built for TikTok — more granular crawl, richer creator context, and tighter dedupe logic on TikTok specifically. BigSpy's TikTok coverage is real but shallower per ad. For TikTok-focused research, the depth gap is significant.

**TikTok Shop product research.** PiPiADS layers in product-level data for TikTok Shop — best-sellers, creator-product pairings, performance metadata. BigSpy doesn't attempt this. For affiliate operators running TikTok Shop offers or dropshippers hunting products, PiPiADS is category-standard.

**Short-form creative-first UX.** PiPiADS's UI is built around TikTok's native creative language — video playback, creator profiles, hashtag context, music metadata. BigSpy's UX is more data-driven and less optimized for video-first browsing.

**Better creator context.** PiPiADS surfaces creator-level data (followers, engagement, past ads) that matter for TikTok influencer-style workflows. BigSpy treats creators more as another filter dimension than a native entity. For TikTok affiliate operators partnering with creators, PiPiADS's context is more useful.

**Depth beats breadth for TikTok-only workflows.** If your entire ad stack is TikTok, paying $77-$263/mo for a TikTok specialist is more valuable than paying $9-$249/mo for a generalist that only gives you TikTok as one of six surfaces.

## Pricing head-to-head

**BigSpy:** Free tier + Basic $9/mo + Pro $99/mo + VIP Enterprise $249/mo (as of April 2026). Year-one: $0 to $2,988.

**PiPiADS:** Starter $77/mo + VIP $155/mo + Pro $263/mo (as of April 2026). Limited free tier. Year-one: $924 to $3,156.

At the entry tier, BigSpy Basic ($9/mo) is $68/mo cheaper than PiPiADS Starter ($77/mo). For operators who want to test ad-spy tooling broadly, BigSpy Basic is hard to beat. For operators who specifically need TikTok depth, the $77/mo PiPiADS Starter is the right price floor.

At the mid tier, BigSpy Pro ($99/mo) and PiPiADS VIP ($155/mo) are comparable — and different products. Pro gives six platforms with general research features. VIP gives TikTok depth with TikTok Shop integration.

At the top tier, BigSpy VIP Enterprise ($249/mo) and PiPiADS Pro ($263/mo) are within $14/mo of each other. The choice is clearly about what research you're doing — cross-platform generalist (BigSpy) or TikTok depth (PiPiADS).

Over three years at steady prices, BigSpy Pro totals $3,564. PiPiADS VIP totals $5,580. The price gap isn't huge — and scope divergence matters more than the dollar difference.

## The affiliate marketer question — is either the right fit?

For TikTok-first affiliates, PiPiADS is close to ideal within its scope. For cross-platform generalist affiliates, BigSpy covers the surfaces. The caveats come when you're doing CPA research specifically.

Neither tool auto-classifies affiliate networks. ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus — none appear as filters in BigSpy or PiPiADS. You're left pasting domain strings and watching them fail when networks rotate subdomains.

Neither tool archives complete landing pages. BigSpy stores destination URLs. PiPiADS stores TikTok-specific landing context. Neither captures the full HTML/CSS/assets of the funnel as a downloadable archive. When the LP goes down or the funnel changes, the historical page is gone.

Neither groups creative concepts across independent advertisers with proof thresholds. If fifteen different advertisers run the same creative, both tools show you fifteen separate ads. There's no "proven concept" filter surfacing creative validated by multiple operators — the signal affiliate buyers value most.

## Final verdict

BigSpy and PiPiADS solve adjacent-but-different problems. BigSpy is a cross-platform generalist — six platforms in one seat at the lowest entry price in the category. PiPiADS is a TikTok specialist — category-leading TikTok Ads Library plus TikTok Shop product research.

If TikTok is one of many platforms you research, BigSpy is the pragmatic choice. You get TikTok coverage alongside five others, and you can always add PiPiADS later if TikTok depth becomes critical.

If TikTok is your platform — or even the majority of your ad spend — PiPiADS is the correct buy. No other tool in the category matches its TikTok depth, and the TikTok Shop integration is unique.
