## TL;DR

**Choose Minea if:** You run dropshipping or DTC e-commerce and need ad visibility across Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, and influencer surfaces with supplier hints and product research. Minea Starter €49/mo, Premium €99/mo, Business €399/mo (as of April 2026). Free tier for tasting. Best for multi-surface product hunters, Shopify operators, and DTC brands.

**Choose PiPiADS if:** You run TikTok Shop offers, TikTok affiliate campaigns, or UGC video creative and need the deepest TikTok Ads Library plus TikTok Shop product intelligence. PiPiADS Starter $77/mo, VIP $155/mo, Pro $263/mo (as of April 2026). Limited free tier. Best for TikTok-first operators.

These tools both serve product research, but on different surface areas. Minea is multi-platform e-commerce. PiPiADS is TikTok specialist. Cross-platform operators sometimes run both.

If your actual job is CPA affiliate research on Meta — ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods — neither tool is built for it. AdRecon is, and we flag it honestly.

## Where Minea wins

**Multi-surface coverage for dropshipping.** Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, and influencer ads in one seat. PiPiADS is effectively TikTok-only. For dropshippers and DTC operators running campaigns across multiple surfaces, Minea consolidates the research into a single tool.

**Facebook coverage.** Minea indexes Facebook ads meaningfully. PiPiADS doesn't. Facebook remains a major channel for DTC brands and dropshippers despite the TikTok surge. If your product research touches Facebook at all, Minea is the only option between these two.

**Pinterest coverage.** Useful for home decor, fashion, lifestyle, and other visual-discovery verticals where Pinterest carries real purchasing intent. PiPiADS doesn't index Pinterest. For e-commerce operators in these niches, Minea's coverage is category-leading.

**Influencer ad surface.** Minea tracks influencer-driven ads as a distinct surface. PiPiADS surfaces TikTok creator ads but doesn't separate out influencer campaigns across platforms. For DTC brands running creator campaigns broadly, Minea's surface is uniquely useful.

**Lower entry price.** Minea Starter at €49/mo (~$52) vs PiPiADS Starter at $77/mo — roughly $22/mo gap, ~$264/year. For operators scaling slowly or testing the category, Minea's entry tier is more approachable.

## Where PiPiADS wins

**Deepest TikTok Ads Library in the category.** PiPiADS is TikTok-specialized with deeper crawl per ad, richer creator context, hashtag-level metadata, and short-form performance signals. Minea's TikTok coverage is part of its multi-surface scope, so depth per TikTok ad is shallower. For TikTok-focused research, PiPiADS wins clearly.

**TikTok Shop product intelligence.** PiPiADS layers in TikTok Shop best-sellers, creator-product pairings, and product-performance metadata. This is the single most differentiated feature between these two tools — Minea has product research, but not the TikTok Shop integration depth PiPiADS ships.

**Better for TikTok creative research.** PiPiADS's video-first UX, creator profiles, and short-form-native design serve TikTok creative ideation directly. Minea's UX is product-card-first and optimized for scanning, not for studying video creative at depth. Different workflows.

**Higher-volume query tiers.** PiPiADS Pro at $263/mo supports serious TikTok research throughput. Minea Business at €399/mo is more expensive but oriented toward e-commerce team features rather than raw research volume.

**Dedicated TikTok community.** PiPiADS is the reference tool among TikTok-first performance operators. Team familiarity, course references, and community tutorials are weighted toward PiPiADS for TikTok-specific workflows. Switching cost matters.

**USD-native pricing.** PiPiADS bills in USD. Minea bills in EUR, which introduces currency conversion math at every renewal for US-based operators. Small friction, but real — a $25/mo gap can widen to $40/mo if exchange rates move against the EUR.

**Creator-first workflow.** If your research question is "which TikTok creators are scaling affiliate offers right now?", PiPiADS answers it with dedicated creator profiles, follower trends, and historical ad logs per creator. Minea surfaces creators as metadata on ads, not as primary research entities.

## Pricing head-to-head

**Minea:** Starter €49/mo + Premium €99/mo + Business €399/mo (as of April 2026). Free tier for tasting. Year-one: €588 to €4,788 — roughly $635 to $5,200 at recent exchange rates.

**PiPiADS:** Starter $77/mo + VIP $155/mo + Pro $263/mo (as of April 2026). Limited free tier. Year-one: $924 to $3,156.

At entry, Minea Starter (~$52) vs PiPiADS Starter ($77) — Minea is ~$25/mo cheaper. For operators running multi-platform dropship research, Minea's entry tier is the cleaner first step.

At mid tier, Minea Premium (~$107) vs PiPiADS VIP ($155) — Minea is ~$48/mo cheaper. Again, you're buying different products: four-surface e-commerce research (Minea) vs deep TikTok research (PiPiADS).

At upper tiers, Minea Business (€399, ~$430) targets serious e-commerce teams. PiPiADS Pro ($263) targets serious TikTok operators. Different audiences, different feature mixes.

Over three years at steady prices, Minea Premium totals ~$3,852. PiPiADS VIP totals $5,580. Minea is meaningfully cheaper across tiers — but only worth it if Minea's multi-surface e-commerce lens matches your use case.

## The affiliate marketer question — is either the right fit?

For CPA affiliate research specifically, neither tool is built for the job.

Neither auto-classifies affiliate networks. ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus — none appear as filterable fields in either product. Minea's product-research filters assume e-commerce context (Shopify stores, supplier SKUs, DTC brands). PiPiADS's filters assume TikTok Shop context. Neither serves the CPA affiliate's "is this ClickBank offer paying?" question.

Neither archives complete landing pages. Minea stores destination URLs with e-commerce metadata. PiPiADS stores TikTok landing context. Neither captures the full HTML/CSS/assets of the funnel as a downloadable archive. When the LP goes down or the funnel changes, the page is gone.

Neither groups creative concepts across independent advertisers with proof thresholds. If fifteen different advertisers run substantially the same creative, both tools show you fifteen separate ads. There's no "proven concept" filter surfacing creatives validated by multiple independent operators.

## Final verdict

Minea and PiPiADS both serve product-research adjacent workflows, but for different buyers on different surfaces. Minea is a multi-platform e-commerce research tool for dropshippers and DTC operators running across Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, and influencer ads. PiPiADS is a TikTok specialist with the deepest TikTok Ads Library plus TikTok Shop integration.

If your dropshipping or DTC work touches more than just TikTok, Minea is the cleaner choice. The supplier hints, multi-surface coverage, and lower price point fit that workflow well.

If your work is concentrated on TikTok — especially TikTok Shop — PiPiADS is the correct specialist buy. No other tool matches its TikTok depth, and the TikTok Shop integration is unique.

For CPA affiliates on Meta, we keep pointing to AdRecon — network classification and landing-page archives are the two jobs neither tool above ships, and those are the jobs affiliate research actually runs on.
