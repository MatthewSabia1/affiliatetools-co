## TL;DR

The affiliate marketer stack is five categories: tracker, ad spy, landing page builder, payment/network infrastructure, and supporting tools (hosting, email, analytics). Each category has several credible vendors. The right stack depends on spend volume, vertical, and platform mix.

At $200-400/month, you can assemble a workable lean stack — free-tier tracker (BeMob), mid-tier ad spy (BigSpy or AdRecon), budget LP builder (Systeme.io or Convertri), and free affiliate network accounts. This scales to $3-10k/month in media spend.

At $500-900/month, you upgrade to Voluum or RedTrack, stack an ad spy specialist with a creative tool, and use a full-featured LP builder. This is the stack of most active affiliates running Meta and native.

At $1,200+/month, you add self-hosted tracking (Binom), multiple ad spy tools, email infrastructure, premium hosting, and specialized tooling per vertical. This is where 6-figure/month operators live.

AdRecon sits in the ad-spy category specifically for Meta-focused affiliate operators. Its $299 lifetime pricing (as of April 2026) is notable because it's one-time, not subscription — which shifts the stack math if you're ad-spy-budget constrained.

This guide walks through each category — what each tool does, which vendors matter, typical pricing as of April 2026, and how to assemble a stack based on your own situation.

## Category 1 — Tracker

A tracker is the system that attributes every click, conversion, and commission back to the creative, audience, LP, and time that drove it. Without a tracker, you're running ads by feel. With one, you're running ads by data.

**What it does:** Receives postbacks from affiliate networks when a conversion fires. Logs the click through a unique identifier. Associates the conversion with the original click's metadata (source, creative ID, LP ID, geo, device, time). Outputs reports by any dimension.

**Why it matters:** Platform pixels (Meta, Google) measure platform-level conversions but often miss affiliate network conversions (cookie delays, cross-device journeys, postback architectures). Trackers close the attribution gap.

**Vendors (April 2026 pricing):**
- **Voluum** — cloud. From $149/month. Industry default. Good reporting, built-in rules, anti-fraud filters.
- **RedTrack** — cloud. From $149/month. Affiliate-focused, strong postback support.
- **BeMob** — cloud with a meaningful free tier (100k events/month). Paid from $49/month. Starting-point favorite.
- **Binom** — self-hosted on your own VPS. ~$179 one-time license + VPS costs ($20-100/month). No per-event fees, unlimited traffic. Favored by high-volume operators.
- **Bemob, FunnelFlux Pro, AdsBridge** — mid-market alternatives.

Pick one. Most affiliates don't stack trackers; one is enough if configured correctly.

## Category 2 — Ad spy tool

The research layer. Covered extensively in our guides on [What Is an Ad Spy Tool](/guides/what-is-an-ad-spy-tool/) and [How to Spy on Facebook Ads](/guides/how-to-spy-on-facebook-ads/).

**What it does:** Surfaces competitor ads with metadata (creative, copy, LP URL, days running, platforms). Good ones add affiliate-network classification, creative grouping, LP archives, and offer aggregation.

**Why it matters:** Shortcuts the expensive part of media buying — guessing what works. A proven ad with 120+ days running on Meta is a data point worth hours of research.

**Vendors (April 2026 pricing):**
- **AdRecon** — Meta-only. $299 one-time/lifetime. Affiliate-network classification, Landing Page Ripper, Proven Creatives, Winning Offers Directory.
- **AdSpy** — Meta-only. $149/month. Boolean search, 10-year historical database.
- **BigSpy** — 7 platforms. Free tier + $9-249/month paid. Breadth over depth.
- **Foreplay** — Meta + TikTok. From $49/month. Creative-strategist UX, swipe files.
- **PowerAdSpy** — 8 platforms. From $49/month. Native + Google Display coverage.
- **PiPiADS** — TikTok specialist. From $77/month.
- **Minea** — dropshipping/TikTok. From €49/month.

For Meta affiliates specifically, AdRecon's one-time pricing is economically distinct — a single $299 payment vs. $149/month AdSpy is $1,489 cheaper over the first year and the gap widens from there.

## Category 3 — Landing page builder

Where clicks become conversions. Affiliates typically need speed of deployment, easy A/B testing, and integrations with trackers.

**What it does:** Drag-and-drop (or template-based) page building, hosting, forms, split testing, and often upsell flows.

**Why it matters:** LP quality determines your conversion rate. Even the best ad can't save a broken LP, and a great LP can rescue a mediocre ad.

**Vendors (April 2026 pricing):**
- **ClickFunnels 2.0** — funnel-style flows. $147-297/month. Best for VSL + upsell structures.
- **LeadsHook** — advertorial and interactive funnels. From $99/month. Strong in Health/Wealth niches.
- **Unbounce** — A/B testing focus. From $99/month. Analytics-heavy.
- **Convertri** — speed-optimized LPs. From $75/month. Favored for fast-loading mobile-first LPs.
- **Systeme.io** — all-in-one budget. Free tier + paid from $27/month. Starting-point favorite.
- **Instapage** — enterprise LP platform. $199+/month. Agency-friendly.
- **WordPress + Elementor** — self-hosted flexibility. $20-50/month hosting + $99/year Elementor Pro. Used heavily for SEO-adjacent funnels.

Most affiliates pick one primary LP builder and stick with it. Switching mid-campaign is expensive.

## Category 4 — Payment and affiliate network infrastructure

Depends on whether you're a publisher (affiliate) or a vendor (product owner).

**If you're a publisher (affiliate):**
Your "payment" infrastructure is the affiliate networks you're registered with.
- **ClickBank** — largest for Health, Wealth, Self-Help info products. Free to join.
- **Digistore24** — EU-friendly alternative with similar catalog. Free.
- **BuyGoods** — growing Health and Wealth network. Free.
- **MaxWeb, WarriorPlus** — specialized networks. Free.
- **MaxBounty, CJ Affiliate, Rakuten** — traditional CPA networks. Free to apply but approval required.

**If you're a vendor (selling your own product):**
- **Fanbasis** — payment gateway built for info products with affiliate management.
- **SamCart** — checkout with upsell and affiliate management. $79-159/month.
- **ThriveCart** — one-time license option. $495 lifetime.
- **Stripe direct + custom affiliate management** — Rewardful, FirstPromoter, or custom.

## Category 5 — Supporting tools

The infrastructure layer that keeps everything running.

**Hosting.** Cloudflare, Vercel, or a traditional host (SiteGround, WP Engine) for LP hosting if self-managing. Plus a CDN for fast LP loads globally.

**Domain management.** Cloudflare DNS, Namecheap, Porkbun. Budget $10-20/year per domain. Most affiliates accumulate 5-20 domains over time.

**Email marketing (optional).** Klaviyo ($45+/month), ActiveCampaign ($29+/month), ConvertKit ($29+/month), MailerLite (free tier). Needed if your offer requires follow-up — high-ticket, consideration-cycle, or info products with sequences.

**Analytics.** Google Analytics 4 (free). Cloudflare Analytics (free). Plausible or Fathom for privacy-focused analytics ($9-14/month).

**Spy-of-spies tools.** SEMrush, Ahrefs, SimilarWeb for competitor keyword and traffic research. Optional but useful. $99-199/month each.

**Creative tools.** Canva (free-$15/month), Figma (free-$15/month), Adobe Creative Cloud ($55/month). For making creative, not analyzing it.

## How to assemble your stack

**Lean stack ($200-400/month, suitable for $3-10k/month media spend):**
- Tracker: BeMob free tier or $49/month.
- Ad spy: AdRecon $299 lifetime, or BigSpy paid tier $9-49/month.
- LP builder: Systeme.io $27/month or Convertri $75/month.
- Network: ClickBank + Digistore24 accounts (free).
- Hosting: Cloudflare + $10 domain.
- Analytics: GA4 (free).

**Medium stack ($500-900/month, suitable for $10-30k/month media spend):**
- Tracker: Voluum or RedTrack $149/month.
- Ad spy: AdRecon $299 lifetime + one specialist (Foreplay $49/month).
- LP builder: LeadsHook $99/month or ClickFunnels $147/month.
- Network: ClickBank, Digistore, BuyGoods, plus 1-2 CPA networks.
- Email: ConvertKit $29/month if running sequences.
- Analytics: GA4 + Plausible $9/month.

**Full pro stack ($1,200+/month, suitable for $50k+/month spend):**
- Tracker: Binom self-hosted ($179 one-time + VPS).
- Ad spy: AdRecon + AdSpy + PiPiADS (if running TikTok) stacked.
- LP builder: Unbounce + WordPress/Elementor.
- Network: Full network portfolio.
- Email: Klaviyo or ActiveCampaign.
- Hosting: WP Engine or Vercel premium.
- Analytics: GA4 + Plausible + Mixpanel for behavior.
- SEO/competitor: Ahrefs or SEMrush.
- Creative: Adobe Creative Cloud + Figma.

## Which ad spy tool should you use in your stack?

For Meta affiliate research specifically, AdRecon is the clearest fit at its $299 one-time price point — affiliate-network classification, LP archive, days-running sort, Winning Offers Directory, Proven Creatives grouping. Meta-only, so stack a TikTok tool (PiPiADS or Minea) if you also run TikTok.

For Boolean URL search over 10-year Meta historical data, AdSpy at $149/month is the specialist for that workflow. Useful if you do heavy pattern research across years of creative.

For creative-strategist workflow built for agencies and brand teams, Foreplay from $49/month is purpose-built with swipe files, briefs, and moodboards. Meta + TikTok coverage.

For multi-platform reconnaissance across Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and native, BigSpy paid tier or PowerAdSpy covers the breadth. Shallower per-platform depth.

The ad spy tool is a research investment that pays back in creative quality. Pick one that matches your actual workflow — breadth if you research widely, depth if you drill into specific niches, affiliate-specific if you're running CPA offers.
