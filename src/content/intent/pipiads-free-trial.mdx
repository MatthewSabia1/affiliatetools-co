## Does PiPiADS have a free trial?

PiPiADS has a **limited free tier** (as of April 2026) rather than a traditional time-limited trial. Sign up with an email (no credit card), get narrow daily query limits on the TikTok database, and use the tool indefinitely at reduced scale. Upgrade to Starter ($77/month) or higher when you need more volume.

This model is similar to BigSpy's and Minea's. It's different from AdSpy (no free access), Foreplay (7-day full-access trial), and PowerAdSpy (7-day trial on select tiers). The tradeoff: PiPiADS free gives you ongoing access to sample data, but caps the daily research volume tightly.

For TikTok-first marketers evaluating PiPiADS, the free tier is enough to confirm whether PiPiADS's TikTok depth beats alternatives like BigSpy's multi-platform coverage or Minea's dropshipping-angle TikTok data.

**Upgrade to Pro ($263/mo) when:**

- You're running enterprise-scale TikTok research across multiple accounts

Most solo TikTok-first operators land on Starter. VIP is for agency or heavy daily usage. Pro is genuinely for teams.

## How PiPiADS's free tier compares to alternatives

**vs AdSpy (no free access):** PiPiADS free is materially better for TikTok evaluation. AdSpy doesn't cover TikTok at all, so the comparison is shaped by platform, not trial.

**vs BigSpy (permanent free tier):** BigSpy free covers TikTok plus 6 other platforms at shallow depth. PiPiADS free covers only TikTok but with specialist depth. For TikTok-first research, PiPiADS free wins on depth; for multi-platform sampling, BigSpy free wins on breadth.

**vs Foreplay (7-day full-access trial):** Different models. Foreplay trial gives 7 days of full access; PiPiADS free gives permanent limited access. Foreplay is creative-workflow focused; PiPiADS is TikTok research focused. Not direct alternatives.

**vs PowerAdSpy (7-day trial on select tiers):** PowerAdSpy's TikTok coverage is shallower than PiPiADS's. For TikTok depth specifically, PiPiADS wins. PowerAdSpy's trial is better for evaluating Native, Google Display, or other non-TikTok platforms.

**vs Minea (limited free tier):** Similar models with different focus. Minea's TikTok coverage is dropshipping-focused; PiPiADS's is broader TikTok research. For dropshipping product hunting on TikTok, Minea; for TikTok ad research more generally, PiPiADS.

**vs AdRecon (no trial, $299 lifetime):** Different platforms entirely. AdRecon is Meta-only. PiPiADS is TikTok-only (for practical purposes). Not alternatives — potentially stack if you work both platforms.
