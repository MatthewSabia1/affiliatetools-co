## Why look beyond Minea?

Minea's pitch is dropshipping-focused ad intelligence — product + supplier research integrated with ad research across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and Influencer ads. Pricing runs €49/month (Starter), €99/month (Premium), and €399/month (Business) as of April 2026. For active dropshippers, Minea is one of the category-aligned specialist tools.

Where Minea falls short: if your actual job isn't dropshipping. Minea doesn't classify affiliate networks, doesn't capture affiliate landing pages, doesn't surface CPA offer intelligence. For affiliate marketers (ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus), TikTok specialists, Meta-only researchers, and creative strategists, specialist alternatives typically deliver better per-dollar value for the specific workflow.

Here are the five Minea alternatives we recommend most often.

## 1. AdRecon — best for affiliate marketers

**Best for:** CPA affiliates running ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, or WarriorPlus offers on Meta.

**Price:** $299 or $599 one-time lifetime (as of April 2026 — confirm at checkout).

Disclosure: we run AdRecon. AdRecon is Meta-only (Facebook, Instagram, Threads — no TikTok, Pinterest, or Influencer ads). It's built for affiliate marketing, a fundamentally different job than Minea's dropshipping focus.

**What AdRecon adds vs Minea:**
- Automatic affiliate-network classification (ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus) via regex-based pattern matching (not AI)
- Landing Page Ripper (Puppeteer + SingleFile) for full HTML/CSS/asset archive downloads
- Winning Lander Vault grouping landers by ads/days-running/network
- Proven Creatives grouping identical creatives across advertisers (15+/30+/60+/90+ proof levels)
- Winning Offers Directory across 300,000+ records
- Days-running profitability signal (120+ days = proven)
- Dead Creative Tracking

**Where Minea still wins:**
- TikTok coverage (AdRecon has none)
- Pinterest coverage (AdRecon has none)
- Influencer ads indexing
- Product + supplier research workflow for dropshipping
- Shopify-adjacent tooling
- EU market depth
- Limited free tier

**Price comparison:** AdRecon $299 lifetime vs Minea Starter €49/mo (~$636/year). AdRecon breaks even after ~6 months. Over 24 months, AdRecon saves ~$973. But only if your job is Meta-only affiliate marketing — otherwise the math is meaningless.

**Verdict:** For affiliate research, AdRecon replaces Minea at a fraction of the cost with affiliate-specific features Minea doesn't have. For dropshipping, Minea's workflow is purpose-built and AdRecon doesn't replace it.

## 2. PiPiADS — best for TikTok specialization

**Best for:** TikTok-first marketers, TikTok Shop sellers, short-form creative specialists.

**Price:** $77/month (Starter), $155/month (VIP), $263/month (Pro) as of April 2026.

PiPiADS is the TikTok specialist. Minea covers TikTok with dropshipping angle; PiPiADS goes deeper with TikTok Shop integration, product-linked creative discovery, and short-form-specific metadata.

**What PiPiADS adds vs Minea:**
- Specialist-grade TikTok database depth
- TikTok Shop product linkage at scale
- Product-to-creative discovery workflow optimized for TikTok
- APAC market coverage broader than Minea
- Short-form creative metadata (hooks, durations, engagement)

**Where Minea still wins:**
- Multi-platform coverage (Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Influencer alongside TikTok)
- Product + supplier research beyond just TikTok
- Dropshipping-specific workflow
- Lower entry price at Starter tier (€49/mo vs $77/mo)
- Shopify integration

**Price comparison:** PiPiADS Starter $924/year vs Minea Starter €588/year (~$636). Minea is cheaper at entry. But PiPiADS's TikTok depth beats Minea's TikTok coverage for TikTok-first work.

**Verdict:** For TikTok-first dropshippers, PiPiADS replaces Minea's TikTok slice with specialist depth. Many operators stack: Minea Starter for multi-platform dropshipping + PiPiADS Starter for TikTok specialization. Typically cheaper than Minea Premium.

## 3. BigSpy — best for budget generalists

**Best for:** First-time ad spy buyers, budget-constrained generalists, multi-platform researchers.

**Price:** Free tier, $9/mo (Basic), $99/mo (Pro), $249/mo (VIP Enterprise) as of April 2026.

BigSpy's permanent free tier and $9/mo Basic are the lowest-risk entries in the ad spy category. For budget-conscious dropshippers or generalists evaluating tools, BigSpy is the cheapest legitimate option.

**What BigSpy adds vs Minea:**
- Permanent free tier — no credit card, no time limit
- Lowest paid entry in the category ($9/mo Basic)
- 7 platforms including YouTube, Twitter that Minea doesn't emphasize
- Broader generalist research angle

**Where Minea still wins:**
- Dropshipping-specific workflow — BigSpy is generalist, not dropshipping-optimized
- Product + supplier research — BigSpy has ads, not products
- Better TikTok and Pinterest data quality for e-commerce specifically
- Shopify integration tooling

**Price comparison:** BigSpy Free vs Minea Starter €49/mo. BigSpy's free tier has no direct comparison. BigSpy Pro $1,188/year vs Minea Premium €1,188/year (~$1,284) — similar at mid-tier with different focus.

**Verdict:** For budget-constrained early-stage dropshippers, start on BigSpy's free tier to learn the category. Upgrade to Minea Starter once you're running active Shopify stores. For generalist research beyond dropshipping, BigSpy's breadth wins.

## 4. AdSpy — best for Meta historical depth

**Best for:** Experienced Meta operators who value Boolean search and historical index.

**Price:** $149/month (as of April 2026).

AdSpy is Meta-only with ~10 years of historical index depth (claims 200M+ ads). For research-focused operators rather than dropshippers, AdSpy delivers Meta depth Minea doesn't target.

**What AdSpy adds vs Minea:**
- Deeper Meta historical index
- Boolean search operators for complex queries
- Comment tracking on competitor ads
- Affiliate-marketing heritage

**Where Minea still wins:**
- TikTok, Pinterest, Influencer coverage (AdSpy is Meta-only)
- Dropshipping-specific workflow
- Product + supplier research
- Limited free tier (AdSpy has no trial)
- Lower effective cost vs AdSpy's $1,788/year

**Price comparison:** AdSpy $1,788/year vs Minea Starter €588/year. Minea is dramatically cheaper at entry tier. AdSpy earns its premium only for Meta-specific research depth.

**Verdict:** For dropshippers, AdSpy is the wrong tool — Meta-only and research-focused rather than dropshipping-workflow optimized. For experienced Meta researchers outside dropshipping, AdSpy's depth matters.

## 5. Foreplay — best for creative strategists

**Best for:** Creative strategists, agency creative teams, in-house brand creative leads.

**Price:** $49/month (Inspiration), $99/month (Agency) as of April 2026.

Foreplay is creative-workflow focused. Minea is dropshipping-research focused. Different jobs — Foreplay builds briefs and manages swipe files; Minea surfaces products and suppliers.

**What Foreplay adds vs Minea:**
- Modern UI with design-tool-adjacent workflows
- Brief builder for client-facing creative presentations
- Swipe files for creative-team workflows
- Team collaboration with permissions
- 7-day full-access free trial

**Where Minea still wins:**
- Dropshipping-specific product research
- Product + supplier linkage
- Pinterest and Influencer ads coverage beyond Foreplay's focus
- Shopify integration tooling
- EU market depth

**Price comparison:** Foreplay Inspiration $588/year vs Minea Starter €588/year. Similar pricing at entry tier, totally different use cases.

**Verdict:** For creative strategy on DTC brand work, Foreplay is the right tool. For dropshipping product research, Minea is the right tool. They don't compete directly — they target different jobs.

## How to pick the right Minea alternative

**If your job is affiliate marketing, not dropshipping:** AdRecon — affiliate-network classification, Landing Page Ripper, and offer intelligence that Minea doesn't provide.

**If TikTok is your primary platform:** PiPiADS — specialist TikTok depth, TikTok Shop integration.

**If budget is the primary constraint:** BigSpy — free tier for risk-free learning, $9/mo Basic for minimum viable research.

**If Meta research depth is critical:** AdSpy — deeper Meta historical index than Minea.

**If creative strategy is your job:** Foreplay — brief builder and swipe files.

**If you want to stay with dropshipping research:** Stay with Minea or evaluate competitors within dropshipping (Dropispy, Peeksta, Ecomhunt). Minea is category leader for this specific job.

## Our verdict

Minea is the right tool for dropshippers and DTC e-commerce operators. The product + supplier research workflow is genuinely category-leading for its focus area. For that persona, Minea's pricing is fair and alternatives don't deliver the same workflow value.

Minea is often wrong-matched for users whose actual job is affiliate marketing, creative strategy, or generalist ad research. The category confusion between "dropshipping ad intelligence" and "affiliate marketing ad intelligence" drives much of this mismatch — these are different jobs with different data needs.

Bottom line: stay with Minea if dropshipping is your job. For affiliate marketing, AdRecon's $299 lifetime is typically the strongest alternative. For TikTok specialization, PiPiADS. For budget, BigSpy. For creative strategy, Foreplay. Self-identify your job first, then pick the matched specialist.
