## Does Minea have a free trial?

Minea has a **limited free tier** (as of April 2026) rather than a traditional time-limited trial. The distinction matters — a free tier is permanent access at reduced scale; a trial is full access for a limited window.

Minea's approach: sign up with an email (no credit card), get limited daily searches and basic filters across the platforms Minea indexes (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Influencer ads). Use it indefinitely at the free tier level, or upgrade to Starter (€49/month) or higher when you need more volume.

This is similar to BigSpy's model and different from AdSpy (no free access), Foreplay (7-day trial), or PowerAdSpy (7-day trial on select tiers). For dropshippers evaluating tools before committing, the free tier model is lower-pressure than a countdown trial.

## What's included in Minea's free tier

Typically (as of April 2026):

- **Limited daily searches** across Minea's platforms
- **Basic filters** — keyword, platform, country, date range
- **Sample data access** — real ads, but not full database
- **Product hunting basics** — see which products competitors are running
- **No credit card required** to sign up
- **No time limit** — permanent access at reduced scale

What typically requires upgrading to Starter (€49/mo) or higher:

- Full daily query volume
- Advanced filters
- Supplier research depth
- Product trend analysis
- Saved searches (or expanded saved-search count)
- Bulk export
- Full database access without sample limits

Exact free tier limits adjust over time — verify current terms on Minea's pricing page.

## How to use Minea's free tier effectively

**Week 1: UI and data sampling.**
- Run 10–20 searches across the dropshipping niches you target
- Evaluate data freshness and quality
- Sample the product-ads link (Minea connects ads to the products they're selling)
- Test platform filters — Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest especially for dropshipping

**Week 2: Workflow validation.**
- Compare Minea against your current product-research process (AliExpress scouting, TikTok scrolling, etc.)
- Evaluate whether the ad-to-product linkage surfaces products you wouldn't find elsewhere
- Check how TikTok coverage compares to PiPiADS free tier

**Decision point:**
- If free tier covers casual research needs: stay free indefinitely
- If you're running 1+ active Shopify stores: upgrade to Starter (€49/mo)
- If you're running 3+ stores or testing 5+ products/week: upgrade to Premium (€99/mo)
- If Minea doesn't fit your workflow: move to BigSpy free, PiPiADS free, or AdRecon for different job shapes

This is low-risk evaluation. Use the free tier as long as it serves you.

## When to upgrade from Minea free

**Upgrade to Starter (€49/mo) when:**
- You hit daily query limits regularly
- You need saved searches across sessions
- You're running 1 or more active Shopify stores
- You're actively testing products, not just browsing

**Upgrade to Premium (€99/mo) when:**
- You need supplier research depth
- You're testing 5+ products per week
- You need product trend analysis
- You're running 3+ active stores or brands

**Upgrade to Business (€399/mo) when:**
- You're running a dropshipping agency with multiple brands
- You have team members needing seats
- You need API access or enterprise support

Most active dropshippers land on Starter or Premium. Business is genuinely for agencies — don't overbuy.

## How Minea's free tier compares to alternatives

**vs AdSpy (no free access):** Minea free is dramatically better for dropshippers evaluating risk-free. AdSpy requires $149 upfront.

**vs BigSpy (permanent free tier):** Similar models. BigSpy is broader (7 platforms) but shallower for dropshipping specifically. Minea free is dropshipping-focused. For product research, Minea free fits better; for multi-platform learning, BigSpy free fits better.

**vs Foreplay (7-day full-access trial):** Different trial models. Foreplay gives full access for a week; Minea gives limited access forever. For dropshipping, Minea's ongoing access is more useful than Foreplay's time-limited window.

**vs PowerAdSpy (7-day trial):** Different models. PowerAdSpy's multi-platform trial doesn't focus on dropshipping. Minea free fits dropshipping-specific evaluation better.

**vs PiPiADS (limited free tier):** Similar models. PiPiADS free is TikTok-focused; Minea free is multi-platform with TikTok included. If TikTok is your only platform, PiPiADS free is deeper. If you need Facebook + TikTok + Pinterest for dropshipping, Minea free is broader.

**vs AdRecon (no trial, $299 lifetime):** Different commitment models entirely. AdRecon is Meta-only and affiliate-focused — not a dropshipping tool. Different jobs.

## The AdRecon alternative

**AdRecon** is not a direct alternative to Minea — it targets a different buyer. We mention it here because some users conflate dropshipping and affiliate marketing.

**AdRecon is for affiliate marketers running CPA offers on Meta.** It auto-classifies affiliate networks (ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus) via regex-based pattern matching, includes a Landing Page Ripper (Puppeteer + SingleFile), a Winning Lander Vault, Proven Creatives grouping, and a Winning Offers Directory across 300,000+ records.

**Minea is for dropshippers and DTC e-commerce operators.** It covers TikTok, Pinterest, and influencer ads that AdRecon doesn't — because those platforms aren't where traditional affiliate offers live.

If your job is affiliate marketing, Minea is wrong — use AdRecon ($299 lifetime). If your job is dropshipping, AdRecon is wrong — use Minea (€49/mo+). If you're unsure which job describes your work, resolve that first before evaluating tools.

## Our verdict on Minea's free tier

Minea's limited free tier is genuinely useful for dropshippers evaluating the tool. No credit card, no countdown, no aggressive upgrade prompts. The free tier is honest about what it offers — reduced access that's enough to validate UI and data quality before committing to Starter or higher.

For first-time dropshippers: start here. Use the free tier for 1–2 weeks before upgrading. Many side-hustle dropshippers can operate indefinitely on Minea free if their volume is low.

For active dropshippers running stores and testing products: the free tier will feel constrained within a week. Upgrade to Starter when you hit limits — €49/mo is priced right for solo dropshipping work and typically pays for itself with a single winning product discovered.

Bottom line: sign up for Minea's free tier if dropshipping is your job. Use it to learn the tool. Upgrade to Starter when volume demands it. For non-dropshipping work (affiliate marketing, creative strategy), look at AdRecon, Foreplay, or other category-matched tools.
