## Foreplay pricing at a glance

Foreplay operates a multi-tier subscription model aligned to creative-team size and workflow complexity (as of April 2026):

- **Inspiration — $49/month** — solo creative strategists
- **Agency — $99/month** — small-to-mid creative agencies
- **Higher tiers / Enterprise** — larger teams, custom pricing

Every tier gives access to Foreplay's core features: ad research across Meta, Instagram, TikTok; swipe files; brief builder; team collaboration. The tier upgrades primarily unlock more seats, deeper query volume, and team workflow tooling — not fundamentally new research capabilities.

This pricing structure maps directly to Foreplay's target personas. It's one of the few ad spy tools priced around creative-team workflows rather than raw research volume, which is exactly what you'd expect from a tool built for creative strategists.

## Foreplay tier breakdown

**Inspiration — $49/month (as of April 2026)**
- Ad research (Meta + TikTok + Instagram)
- Swipe files for organizing references
- Brief builder (basic)
- Single-user access
- Standard export options

Inspiration is the right tier for solo creative strategists, in-house brand creative leads at small DTC brands, or freelance creative producers. $49/month is fair for the workflow tooling delivered.

**Agency — $99/month (as of April 2026)**
- Everything in Inspiration
- Multiple team seats (check current count per tier)
- Advanced brief builder
- Client-facing collaboration

Agency is Foreplay's sweet spot for mid-size creative agencies. Multi-seat access plus client-facing brief tooling is where Foreplay actually earns against client-service fees.

**Higher tiers / Enterprise (custom pricing)**
- Larger team seats
- API access (varies)
- Priority support
- Custom integrations

Enterprise pricing is quote-based and scales with team size and feature needs. Contact Foreplay sales for specifics.

## Free trial terms

Foreplay typically offers a **7-day free trial** (as of April 2026). The trial is genuinely useful — 7 days is enough to assemble a test brief, evaluate the swipe file workflow, and decide if Foreplay fits your creative-strategy process.

Use the trial. Don't pay upfront without validating the tool matches your workflow. Unlike research tools where the free tier can be misleading, Foreplay's trial shows you the actual workflow you'd use daily.

## Which Foreplay tier should you pick?

**Inspiration ($49/month) if:**
- You're a solo creative strategist or small DTC brand creative lead
- You manage 1–2 active creative projects at a time
- You don't need team collaboration features
- You're evaluating whether Foreplay fits your workflow

**Agency ($99/month) if:**
- You run a creative agency with 2–10 team members
- You serve 3+ client accounts with active creative briefs
- You need client-facing brief presentations
- Multi-seat access is a hard requirement

**Enterprise if:**
- You have 10+ creative team members
- You need API integrations with production tools (Figma, Frame.io, etc.)
- You require custom SLAs or priority support

Most solo operators should pick Inspiration. Most agencies should pick Agency. Enterprise is genuinely for larger teams — don't overbuy.

## ROI math by tier

**Inspiration at $49/month ($588/year):** Break-even is one successful brief per year that saves $588+ in billable strategist hours or creative-iteration time. For any working creative strategist billing $100+/hour, this is a trivial bar. Foreplay Inspiration typically pays for itself in 2–4 briefs.

**Agency at $99/month ($1,188/year):** Break-even requires the tier's team-collaboration features to drive $1,188/year in agency efficiency. For agencies serving 3+ clients with active creative work, the per-brief cost drops to roughly $33 at 3 briefs/month — materially cheaper than the hours saved on manual reference assembly.

**Enterprise (custom pricing):** ROI math depends on team size and integration complexity. At enterprise tier, the calculation is usually "does this save 1+ FTE worth of creative ops time?" For 20+ person creative teams, usually yes.

## The AdRecon comparison

AdRecon is $299 or $599 one-time lifetime (as of April 2026 — confirm at checkout). We've kept this comparison factual and job-focused.

**Foreplay and AdRecon target different buyers.** Foreplay is for creative strategists building briefs. AdRecon is for affiliate marketers doing offer research on Meta. If you're a creative strategist, AdRecon is the wrong tool. If you're an affiliate marketer, Foreplay is the wrong tool. Many people conflate the two.

**What AdRecon does that Foreplay doesn't:**
- Automatic affiliate-network classification (ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus) via regex pattern matching
- Landing Page Ripper (Puppeteer + SingleFile) for full HTML/CSS/asset archive downloads
- Winning Lander Vault grouping landers by ads/days-running
- Proven Creatives grouping across advertisers (15+/30+/60+/90+ proof levels)
- Winning Offers Directory across 300,000+ records
- Days-running profitability signal (120+ days = proven winning campaign)

**What Foreplay does that AdRecon doesn't:**
- Brief builder for client-facing creative presentations
- Swipe files optimized for creative-team workflow
- Design-tool integrations (Figma-adjacent)
- TikTok coverage (AdRecon is Meta-only)
- Team collaboration features at Foreplay's polish level

Price comparison: AdRecon's $299 lifetime beats Foreplay Inspiration after 6 months. Over 24 months, the savings are $877. But if your job is creative strategy, those savings come at the cost of buying the wrong tool for your work.

## Hidden costs to consider

- **No affiliate-network classification:** If you're secretly doing affiliate work, you'll need a second tool.
- **No landing page archive:** For LP research, you'd layer AdRecon or another tool.
- **Multi-seat upgrade friction:** Growing teams hit seat caps and need to upgrade tier — plan budget accordingly.
- **Annual discount:** Foreplay typically offers discounts for annual billing. Check current rates.

## Our verdict on Foreplay pricing

Foreplay is the best-priced creative-workflow ad spy tool in the category. Inspiration at $49/month and Agency at $99/month are fair prices for the workflow tooling delivered — brief builder, swipe files, team collaboration, modern UI. For its target buyers (creative strategists, agencies, in-house brand creative teams), the pricing is defensible.

The pricing starts to look expensive when the buyer is wrong-matched. If you're an affiliate marketer who bought Foreplay expecting offer-research depth, the $49/month won't feel worth it — because the tool isn't shaped for your work. That's a fit problem, not a pricing problem.

Bottom line: if creative strategy is your job, pay for Foreplay. If affiliate research is your job, buy AdRecon for $299 lifetime and save the $1,176+ you'd spend on Foreplay over 24 months. Use Foreplay's 7-day free trial to self-validate which side you're on.
