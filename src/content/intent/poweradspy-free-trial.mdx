## Does PowerAdSpy have a free trial?

**Yes, typically — on select tiers.** PowerAdSpy has historically offered a 7-day free trial on some of its subscription tiers (as of April 2026). Trial availability has varied over the years and across tiers, so verify current terms on PowerAdSpy's pricing page before committing.

This puts PowerAdSpy in the middle of the ad spy trial landscape. AdSpy has no trial at all. BigSpy has a permanent free tier. Foreplay typically offers a 7-day trial like PowerAdSpy. For first-time buyers, a 7-day trial is enough to validate whether PowerAdSpy's multi-platform coverage matches your workflow.

Note: PowerAdSpy's trial typically requires a credit card and auto-converts to paid at trial end. If you don't want to continue, cancel before the 7-day window closes.

## Trial gotchas to watch for

**Auto-conversion to paid.** PowerAdSpy's trials typically convert to paid subscriptions automatically at trial end. Set a calendar reminder for day 6 if you're unsure.

**Credit card required.** The trial signup almost always requires a card on file. This is standard for trials but means you should budget for potential charges if you miss the cancellation window.

**Tier-specific trials.** Not every tier offers a trial. If you start on a free trial for Basic but want to test Premium features, you may need to upgrade mid-trial (which typically still charges at trial end).

**Refund terms vary.** Even if you cancel late, refund eligibility is at PowerAdSpy's discretion. Document trial usage and cancellation timing carefully.

**Annual plan trials.** If the trial converts to annual billing, you may be locked into a larger payment. Verify whether the trial converts to monthly or annual before signing up.
