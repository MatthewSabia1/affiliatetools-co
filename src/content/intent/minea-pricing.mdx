## Minea pricing at a glance

Minea runs a three-tier subscription model plus a limited free tier (as of April 2026):

- **Free tier** — limited, permanent, for sampling
- **Starter — €49/month** — solo dropshippers
- **Premium — €99/month** — active e-commerce operators
- **Business — €399/month** — dropshipping agencies / teams

All paid tiers cover Minea's full platform set: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and Influencer ads. Tier differences are query volume, research depth, and team features — not platform access.

Minea bills in EUR. US-based buyers should factor exchange rates: €49 ≈ $53, €99 ≈ $107, €399 ≈ $430 as of April 2026 (rates fluctuate). Budget in your local currency accordingly.

## Minea tier breakdown

**Free tier — €0 (as of April 2026)**
- Limited daily searches
- Basic filters
- Access to sample data across platforms
- No credit card required to start

The free tier is genuinely useful for sampling the tool and validating UI fit. Not deep enough for real dropshipping research but honest about what it delivers.

**Starter — €49/month (as of April 2026)**
- Full platform access (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Influencer)
- Product research workflow
- Basic filters and search
- Limited daily query volume
- Single-user access

Starter is priced for solo dropshippers running 1–3 active Shopify stores. At €49/month, it's comparable to Foreplay Inspiration and PowerAdSpy Basic — but purpose-built for dropshipping rather than general creative or multi-platform research.

**Premium — €99/month (as of April 2026)**
- Everything in Starter
- Expanded query volume
- Advanced filters
- Supplier research depth
- Product trend analysis
- More saved searches

Premium is the "real work" tier for active dropshippers testing 5+ products per week. The supplier research depth and product trend analysis earn the upgrade for serious operators.

**Business — €399/month (as of April 2026)**
- Full platform depth
- Team seats (check current count)
- API access (verify on current pricing page)
- Priority support
- Enterprise features

Business is priced for dropshipping agencies, multi-brand teams, or enterprise e-commerce operators. Solo dropshippers rarely need this tier.

## Which Minea tier should you pick?

**Free tier if:**
- You're evaluating whether Minea fits your workflow
- You're not yet committed to dropshipping as a business
- You want to sample the data before spending

**Starter (€49/mo) if:**
- You're a solo dropshipper with 1–3 active stores
- You test 1–5 new products per month
- You're early-stage but serious

**Premium (€99/mo) if:**
- You're running active stores with product rotation
- You test 5+ products per week
- You need supplier research and trend analysis

**Business (€399/mo) if:**
- You manage 10+ stores or brands
- You have team members needing seats
- You need API access or enterprise SLAs

Most active solo dropshippers should be on Premium. Starter is for early-stage or part-time operators. Business is genuinely for agencies.

## ROI math by tier

**Free tier:** Infinite positive ROI — it's free. Any insight extracted = pure upside.

**Starter at €49/month (€588/year):** Break-even requires one successful product discovered via Minea per year that generates €588+ profit. For any working dropshipper, this is trivial — most successful dropship products clear €500+ margin in their first profitable week.

**Premium at €99/month (€1,188/year):** Break-even requires the tier's deeper product research to drive €600+/year in additional product discovery value. For dropshippers testing 5+ products weekly, the supplier research alone usually earns this back by reducing sourcing research time.

**Business at €399/month (€4,788/year):** Break-even requires enterprise-scale operations. If you're running 10+ brands or a dropshipping agency, the math works. Below that scale, you're overpaying.

## The AdRecon comparison

AdRecon is $299 or $599 one-time lifetime (as of April 2026 — confirm at checkout).

**Minea and AdRecon target different buyers.** Minea is for dropshippers doing product research. AdRecon is for affiliate marketers doing offer research on Meta. Different jobs, different tools. If you're conflating dropshipping and affiliate marketing in your planning, pause and resolve that first.

**What AdRecon does that Minea doesn't:**
- Automatic affiliate-network classification (ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, WarriorPlus) via regex pattern matching — Minea doesn't classify affiliate networks
- Landing Page Ripper for affiliate funnels (Puppeteer + SingleFile archives)
- Winning Lander Vault grouping landers by linked ads
- Proven Creatives grouping across advertisers with proof levels
- Winning Offers Directory aggregating 300,000+ records including affiliate offers
- Dead Creative Tracking

**What Minea does that AdRecon doesn't:**
- TikTok coverage (AdRecon is Meta-only)
- Pinterest coverage
- Product + supplier research workflow for dropshipping
- Influencer ads indexing
- EU market depth
- Shopify-adjacent tooling

Price comparison: AdRecon's $299 lifetime beats Minea Starter after roughly 6 months. Over 24 months, savings are ~$973 — but only if your job is Meta-only affiliate marketing. If your job is dropshipping, AdRecon can't replace Minea.

## Hidden costs to consider

- **EUR billing:** USD budgets should factor exchange rate volatility.
- **No affiliate-network classification:** If you pivot from dropshipping to CPA affiliate work, Minea doesn't surface the intel you'd need.
- **No landing page ripper:** For affiliate funnel research, you'd layer a second tool.
- **Starter query limits:** Growth will push you toward Premium — plan for it.

## Our verdict on Minea pricing

Minea's pricing is fair for its target buyer. Starter at €49/month is the right entry point for solo dropshippers. Premium at €99/month is the working tier for active operators. Business at €399/month is honestly priced for agencies.

Where pricing gets muddled: when non-dropshippers buy Minea expecting affiliate or creative-workflow features. The tool is purpose-built for dropshipping. If you're an affiliate marketer or a creative strategist, you're wrong-matched — buy AdRecon or Foreplay respectively.

Bottom line: yes for active dropshippers and DTC operators, particularly those with TikTok or Pinterest exposure and EU market focus. Validate via the limited free tier. Budget Premium for serious operations. For non-dropshipping use cases, look elsewhere.
