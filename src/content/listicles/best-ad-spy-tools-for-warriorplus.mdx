WarriorPlus is heavy in BizOpp, Wealth, and digital-product offers. These verticals have specific creative conventions — long-form sales pages, urgency-heavy angles, testimonial-stacked funnels — that research tools without network classification can't filter cleanly.

Generic tools return everything in a search, and you manually identify which creatives are WarriorPlus by URL pattern. AdRecon auto-classifies. **Best for:** WarriorPlus affiliates running Meta campaigns.

AdRecon's classifier detects WarriorPlus creatives via URL pattern matching. Classified creatives flow into the Ad Feed (filterable by WarriorPlus network tag), the Winning Lander Vault (landing pages linked to WarriorPlus Meta ads), Proven Creatives (cross-advertiser grouping with proof tiers: 15+, 30+, 60+, 90+ linked ads), and the Winning Offers Directory (every WarriorPlus offer aggregated with days-running, active-ad counts, niche tagging).

Sort by days running descending to surface creatives 120+ days (proven profitable). Open Proven Creatives → see which WarriorPlus sales angles are being cloned across advertisers. Click into the top offer → Landing Page Ripper downloads the full sales funnel for offline teardown.

Classifier is regex-based, not AI. Deterministic behavior.

**Where AdRecon loses points:** Meta-only. No TikTok WarriorPlus coverage. Smaller historical archive than AdSpy.

**Why #1:** WarriorPlus auto-classification is the load-bearing feature. No other tool has it.
