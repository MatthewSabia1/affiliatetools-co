## TL;DR
For Meta-based crypto offers, AdRecon is the top choice due to its landing page ripping capabilities. If you are focusing on TikTok, PiPiADS is the only serious option. For those needing deep historical archives or Native ad coverage, AdSpy and PowerAdSpy respectively fill those gaps.

## Why this list exists
Crypto is one of the hardest verticals to scale. Between strict compliance filters and the constant cycle of ad account bans, the ad creative is only half the battle. The real "secret sauce" is the bridge page. Most spy tools show you the ad but leave you guessing what happens after the click. This list focuses on tools that actually help you reverse-engineer the entire funnel.

## How we ranked
We didn't rely on marketing brochures. We tested these tools using actual crypto keywords and offers, scoring them across eight dimensions: platform coverage, data freshness, price, UX, landing page availability, offer classification, affiliate focus, and search granularity.

## The ranking

### 1. AdRecon — The best for Meta crypto offers

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

When running crypto on Meta, the bridge page is everything. AdRecon wins here because it doesn't just show you the ad; it includes a Landing Page Ripper that lets you download the full HTML/CSS and assets as a ZIP. This is critical for analyzing how competitors are framing their "wealth" angles to avoid the ban-hammer.

As a disclosure, While we built it specifically for the affiliate workflow—including classification for networks like MaxWeb and BuyGoods—we aren't the biggest player. AdRecon is a newer tool (launched 2026) and lacks the decade-long historical archive that a tool like AdSpy possesses.

It is priced at $299 / $599 lifetime (as of April 2026). For a solo operator, the one-time payment is a massive advantage over monthly subscriptions.

<Screenshot slug="adrecon" variant="hero" />

### 2. PiPiADS — The gold standard for TikTok crypto research

<ToolLogo slug="pipiads" size="lg" showName />

If your strategy involves TikTok, you can't rely on generalist tools. PiPiADS has the deepest TikTok database in the category. Crypto creatives on TikTok move fast and rely heavily on UGC (User Generated Content) and "get rich" hooks that are highly platform-specific.

The tool integrates well with the TikTok Creative Center, making it easy to spot trending formats. However, it is weak on Meta and offers no landing page archiving, meaning you'll have to manually click through to see the funnels.

Pricing starts at $77/mo for the Starter plan (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="pipiads" variant="hero" />

### 3. AdSpy — Unmatched historical data for Meta

<ToolLogo slug="adspy" size="lg" showName />

AdSpy is the "old guard." Its biggest strength is a 10-year historical database. If you want to see how crypto angles have evolved since the 2017 or 2021 booms to find "forgotten" hooks that still work, this is the tool.

The Boolean search (AND/OR/NOT) is significantly more powerful than the basic filters found in newer tools. The downside is the price—$149/mo (as of April 2026)—and a dated UX that feels like a relic from 2015. It also lacks landing page archives, which is a major hurdle for crypto affiliates.

<Screenshot slug="adspy" variant="hero" />

### 4. PowerAdSpy — Best for Native and Diversified Traffic

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

Crypto doesn't just live on social media. Some of the highest-converting crypto offers run on Native networks like Taboola and Outbrain. PowerAdSpy is the only tool on this list that provides meaningful coverage of Native ads alongside Meta and Google Display.

If you are a media buyer running a multi-surface campaign, this is your best bet. The trade-off is depth; because they cover so many platforms, their data on any single platform is thinner than what you'll find in a specialist tool.

Plans range from $49/mo to $249/mo (as of April 2026).

### 5. BigSpy — A low-cost entry point

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

BigSpy is for the operator who isn't ready to drop $150+ a month. It offers a real free tier (no credit card required), which is rare in this industry. It provides a broad look at Meta, TikTok, and Pinterest.

While it's great for sampling the market, it's shallow. You won't find the granular affiliate-network classification or the landing page tools needed to actually scale a crypto offer. It's a research tool, not a scaling tool.

Pricing starts with a Free tier and goes up to a VIP Enterprise plan at $249/mo (as of April 2026).

## How to pick
If you are running Meta and need to see the exact bridge pages to avoid bans, go with AdRecon. If you are exclusively on TikTok, PiPiADS is the only choice. For those running Native ads or needing a 10-year history of Meta ads, PowerAdSpy and AdSpy are the professional standards.
