## TL;DR
For Meta-focused BizOpp affiliates, AdRecon is the clear winner due to its network-specific filtering. If you need a decade of historical data, go with AdSpy; for Native ad scaling, PowerAdSpy is the best fit.

## Why this list exists
BizOpp (Business Opportunity) and Make Money Online (MMO) offers are some of the hardest to track because they move fast and often use aggressive cloaking or short-lived landing pages. Most general ad spy tools treat a BizOpp ad the same as a t-shirt ad. To actually make money in this niche, you need to know which affiliate network is powering the offer and whether the landing page is actually converting.

## How we ranked
We didn't just look at feature lists. We ran these tools through a gauntlet of 8 weighted dimensions: coverage, dataFreshness, price, ux, landingPages, offers, affiliateFocus, and search. We prioritized tools that help you identify "winning" patterns specifically for CPA offers rather than general e-commerce.

## The ranking

### 1. AdRecon — The affiliate marketer's Meta-only ad intelligence stack

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

AdRecon is built for the specific workflow of a CPA affiliate. Instead of guessing if an ad is for a BizOpp offer, AdRecon uses affiliate-network classification to tag ads from ClickBank, Digistore24, BuyGoods, MaxWeb, and WarriorPlus. This allows you to filter by the network you actually use, cutting out the noise of DTC brands.

The standout feature here is the Landing Page Ripper. In the BizOpp world, landing pages get flagged and deleted constantly. AdRecon lets you download the full HTML/CSS and assets as a ZIP, so you have the blueprint even after the original page goes dark. It also provides a "days-running" signal, which is the most honest proxy for profitability in this niche.

The trade-off is scope. AdRecon is Meta-only. It does not cover TikTok, YouTube, or Google. It's also a newer tool (launched 2026), so it doesn't have the decade-long archive that some competitors boast. Pricing is a one-time fee of $299 or $599 for lifetime access (as of April 2026). Since

### 2. AdSpy — The 10-year Meta ad archive with Boolean search

<ToolLogo slug="adspy" size="lg" showName />

If you need to see how a specific BizOpp angle evolved over the last five years, AdSpy is the tool. Their database is massive and their Boolean search (AND/OR/NOT) is far more powerful than the simple filters found in most other tools.

For a senior operator, the ability to exclude certain keywords while targeting others is vital for cleaning up BizOpp search results. However, the UX is dated, and the $149/mo recurring cost (as of April 2026) is a steep monthly overhead compared to lifetime options.

The biggest weakness for BizOpp operators is the lack of landing page archiving. You get the link, but if the affiliate has been banned or the offer has rotated, you're looking at a 404 page.

### 3. PowerAdSpy — Broadest multi-platform coverage, including Native

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

Many high-ticket BizOpp offers eventually migrate from Meta to Native networks like Taboola or Outbrain to avoid strict compliance triggers. PowerAdSpy is the best choice for this specific transition because it covers nearly every surface, including Reddit and Quora.

While its depth on any single platform is thinner than a specialist tool, the breadth is its strength. If you are running a multi-channel campaign for an MMO offer, this is your hub. The pricing ranges from $49/mo to $249/mo (as of April 2026).

The downside is the interface, which feels like a relic from 2015, and a total lack of affiliate-network classification. You'll spend a lot of time manually sifting through ads to find the actual CPA offers.

<Screenshot slug="poweradspy" variant="hero" />

### 4. BigSpy — Multi-platform ad spy with a real free tier

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

BigSpy is the "sampler" tool. It's the only reputable option that offers a real free tier without requiring a credit card upfront. For a beginner trying to understand what a "BizOpp" ad even looks like, this is the place to start.

It covers Meta, TikTok, and Pinterest, making it a decent generalist tool. However, the free tier has hard limits that will frustrate a professional operator. The paid tiers range from $9/mo to $249/mo (as of April 2026).

Like PowerAdSpy, it lacks landing page archiving and network classification. It's a great tool for spotting trends, but not for reverse-engineering a full funnel.

### 5. PiPiADS — The TikTok ad specialist

<ToolLogo slug="pipiads" size="lg" showName />

TikTok has become a goldmine for "side hustle" and BizOpp offers, specifically those targeting Gen Z and Millennials. PiPiADS is the undisputed leader for TikTok data. If your strategy involves UGC-style videos promoting an MMO offer, you need this tool.

It integrates with the TikTok Creative Center and provides deep insights into TikTok-specific ad performance. Pricing sits between $77/mo and $155/mo (as of April 2026).

The limitation is obvious: it's weak on Meta and offers zero help with landing page archiving or network-specific filtering. It's a specialist tool for a specialist platform.

## How to pick
If you live and breathe Meta CPA offers on networks like MaxWeb or ClickBank, AdRecon's network tags and LP Ripper make it the only logical choice. If you are a power researcher who needs deep historical archives and complex search queries, AdSpy is the way to go. For those scaling into Native ads, stick with PowerAdSpy.
