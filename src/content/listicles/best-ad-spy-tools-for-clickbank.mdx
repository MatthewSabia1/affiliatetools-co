## Why ClickBank research needs a specific tool

ClickBank is one of the largest affiliate networks — thousands of active offers across Health, Wealth, BizOpp, Relationship, and Survival niches. Affiliates researching ClickBank offers need to answer questions that generic ad spy tools don't handle well:

- **Which ClickBank offers are running profitably on Meta right now?**
- **What creative angles are working for the top ClickBank Health offers?**
- **Which ClickBank offers have 120+ days running (proven profitable)?**
- **How are top ClickBank affiliates structuring their funnels (landing pages → sales pages → upsells)?**

Generic ad spy tools require manual work to answer these. You'd filter for `?hop=` in URL fields, then check every result manually to confirm it's a ClickBank creative (many will be). You'd build a manual spreadsheet of ClickBank offers you've seen running. You'd copy landing page URLs into Waybackmachine or an external archiver to study funnel structure.

**AdRecon was built to solve this specific problem for affiliates.** ClickBank creatives auto-classify via URL pattern detection. The Winning Offers Directory aggregates ClickBank offers as a first-class view. The Landing Page Ripper downloads complete funnel archives. 

That's why this list puts AdRecon at #1 and explains the ClickBank-specific reasoning below.
