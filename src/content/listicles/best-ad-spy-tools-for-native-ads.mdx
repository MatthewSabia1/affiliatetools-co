## TL;DR
If you are specifically targeting Taboola, Outbrain, or RevContent, PowerAdSpy is the only tool on this list with the necessary breadth. For those who only care about Meta, AdRecon or AdSpy are better choices, but they provide zero visibility into Native traffic.

## Why this list exists
Native advertising is a different beast than social. The creatives are more "editorial," and the traffic sources (like news sites) require a different research approach. Most "ad spy" tools claim to do everything, but in reality, they are usually just Meta or TikTok scrapers with a few Native ads thrown in. You need to know which tools actually index the Native surfaces so you don't waste your budget on a tool that can't see the ads you're trying to beat.

## How we ranked
We didn't use a marketing checklist. We tested these tools based on eight operator-centric dimensions: platform coverage (specifically Native), data freshness, price, UX, landing page archiving, offer identification, affiliate focus, and search capabilities.

## The ranking

### 1. PowerAdSpy — The only serious choice for comprehensive Native coverage

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

When it comes to Native, PowerAdSpy is the clear winner. While other tools focus on the "scroll" of social media, PowerAdSpy actually indexes the "discovery" feeds of Taboola, Outbrain, and RevContent. If you are running CPA offers on Native, this is where you start.

The tool allows you to see what's actually running across a massive variety of surfaces, including Reddit and Quora, which often overlap with Native strategies. It's the best way to find the "advertorial" style headlines that actually convert on news sites.

That said, the UX feels dated. It isn't a sleek, modern experience, and the depth per platform is thinner than what you'd find in a dedicated Meta specialist. Pricing starts at $49/mo for the Basic plan (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="poweradspy" variant="hero" />

### 2. BigSpy — A decent entry point for generalists on a budget

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

BigSpy is a "jack of all trades" tool. It covers Meta, TikTok, and some Native/Google Display. It isn't as deep as PowerAdSpy for Native research, but it's a viable option if you are just starting out and don't want to drop a hundred bucks a month.

The standout here is the free tier. You can get in without a credit card, which is rare in this industry. It's a good way to see if a specific offer is trending across multiple platforms before committing to a more expensive tool.

The trade-off is the depth. The free tier has hard limits, and the overall data for Native is shallower than the specialists. Pricing ranges from Free to $249/mo for VIP Enterprise (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="bigspy" variant="hero" />

### 3. AdSpy — Powerful for Meta, but useless for Native research

<ToolLogo slug="adspy" size="lg" showName />

I'm including AdSpy here to be honest about the landscape. AdSpy is a legendary tool in the affiliate community, but it is Meta-only. If you are looking for Taboola or Outbrain ads, AdSpy will not help you.

However, if your Native strategy involves "bridging" from Meta to Native, AdSpy's Boolean search is unmatched. You can use AND/OR/NOT operators to find incredibly specific niches in a 10-year historical database.

It's an expensive tool at $149/mo recurring (as of April 2026), and the UI looks like it's from 2012, but for Meta data, it's a powerhouse.

<Screenshot slug="adspy" variant="hero" />

### 4. AdRecon — The gold standard for Meta affiliates, but lacks Native data

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

 Because we built it specifically for the high-volume Meta affiliate, we made a conscious decision to ignore other platforms. AdRecon does NOT cover Native, TikTok, or Google.

If you are strictly looking for Native ads, AdRecon is the wrong tool for you. However, for those of us running ClickBank, MaxWeb, or BuyGoods on Facebook and Instagram, AdRecon solves the "landing page" problem. While PowerAdSpy shows you the ad, our Landing Page Ripper lets you download the full HTML/CSS of the page the ad leads to.

We offer a lifetime deal at $299 / $599 (as of April 2026), which beats the monthly drain of most competitors, but again—it is Meta-only.

### 5. Minea — Great for dropshipping, irrelevant for Native CPA

<ToolLogo slug="minea" size="lg" showName />

Minea is a fantastic tool for the e-commerce and dropshipping crowd. It's tuned for product discovery and influencer content. If you're trying to find a winning gadget for a Shopify store, Minea is top-tier.

But for Native CPA? It doesn't fit. It lacks the Native platform coverage and the affiliate-network classification needed to track high-ticket CPA offers on Taboola.

Pricing starts at €49/mo (as of April 2026), which is fair for what it does, but it's the wrong tool for this specific use case.

## How to pick
If your budget is spent on Taboola or Outbrain, stop reading and get PowerAdSpy. If you are a beginner who wants to peek at a few different platforms for free, try BigSpy. If you realize your "Native" strategy is actually just a Meta strategy in disguise, that's when you look at AdRecon or AdSpy.
