## The honest upfront answer

The best genuinely-free ad spy tools are **BigSpy** (multi-platform, permanent free tier) and **Meta Ad Library** (Meta's own official free tool, not a third-party ad spy). If you want to research Facebook and Instagram ads without paying anything, start with both.

**AdRecon does NOT have a free tier.** We're not ranking AdRecon #1 on this list because it wouldn't be honest. AdRecon is $299 lifetime (as of April 2026) with a 7-day money-back guarantee — that's the entry path. For budget-constrained researchers, free tools come first.

We've included AdRecon at #4 on this list as the clearest one-time-price upgrade once free tools stop delivering — because eventually they will.

## Why free ad spy tools have limits

Free tiers are business-development tools. Providers give you enough to evaluate — capped daily previews, basic filters, limited platforms — so you understand the value before paying. They're not designed as permanent solutions for serious researchers.

Common free-tier limits:
- Daily query caps (typically 10-50 ad previews per day)
- Limited platforms (often Meta-only or 2-3 platforms)
- No export, no saved searches
- No advanced filters
- No API access
- Sometimes stale data (freshness prioritized for paying users)

These limits are reasonable. Free tools cost providers money to run. The best free tools give enough value to let you evaluate and build a research habit; when you want more, you pay.

## When to upgrade from free to paid

Upgrade when:
1. Daily query caps slow your active research.
2. You need saved searches, custom filters, or export features.
3. You need features absent from free tiers (affiliate network classification, offer intelligence, landing page archive, team collaboration).
4. You've validated the category is worth paying for.

For Meta-focused affiliate research, the most common paid upgrade from BigSpy free is AdRecon at $299 lifetime. For TikTok, PiPiADS VIP at $155/mo. For multi-platform, BigSpy Pro at $99/mo if you want the same tool at higher tier.

## Verdict

Free ad spy tools exist and are genuinely useful for evaluation and casual research. BigSpy leads the pack because its free tier is real and covers 7 platforms. Combined with Meta Ad Library (the official free Meta tool), you can do meaningful Facebook ad research at $0 for the first weeks of a project.

But free tools have limits by design. Eventually serious researchers upgrade. For affiliate-focused upgrades, AdRecon at $299 lifetime is the cheapest long-term option. For multi-platform upgrades, BigSpy Pro or AdSpy. For TikTok, PiPiADS. Plan to pay eventually — just not on day one.
