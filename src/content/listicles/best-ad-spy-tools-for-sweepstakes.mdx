## TL;DR
For Meta-focused sweepstakes, AdRecon is the top pick due to its landing page ripper and network classification. If you need Native (Taboola/Outbrain), go with PowerAdSpy; for TikTok-specific sweeps, PiPiADS is the standard.

## Why this list exists
Sweepstakes is a high-churn vertical. What works today—whether it's a "mystery box" or a "gift card giveaway"—is usually dead in two weeks. To survive, you can't guess; you have to see what's currently spending. The problem is that most spy tools are built for e-commerce, not CPA affiliates. You need to know not just the creative, but the exact landing page and the network fueling the offer.

## How we ranked
We evaluated these tools based on eight specific dimensions: platform coverage, data freshness, pricing structure, user experience (UX), landing page archiving capabilities, offer identification, affiliate-specific focus, and search granularity.

## The ranking

### 1. AdRecon — The most efficient way to rip sweeps landing pages and track Meta offers

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

In the sweeps vertical, the landing page is where the money is made. AdRecon wins here because it doesn't just show you the ad; it lets you download the full HTML/CSS and assets of the landing page as a ZIP. This removes the guesswork from rebuilding high-converting funnels.

It also solves the "keyword" problem. Instead of searching for "iPhone giveaway" and sifting through junk, you can filter by affiliate networks like MaxWeb or BuyGoods. This lets you see exactly what the top CPA players are running. It only covers Meta (Facebook, Instagram, Threads). If you need TikTok or Google, you'll need a second tool.

It launched in 2026, so it doesn't have the decade-long archive that some competitors boast. It's built for the current Meta landscape, not for historical research. Pricing is $299 for a monthly plan or a $599 lifetime option (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="adrecon" variant="hero" />

### 2. PowerAdSpy — Essential for those scaling sweeps across Taboola and Outbrain

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

If your sweepstakes strategy involves Native ads, PowerAdSpy is a necessity. While most tools focus on the "scroll" of social media, PowerAdSpy covers the "discovery" feeds of Taboola, Outbrain, and RevContent. Sweeps often perform exceptionally well on Native, and this is the best place to find those specific headlines and images.

The downside is that the depth per platform is thinner than a specialist tool. The UX also feels a bit dated compared to modern SaaS. It's a "wide but shallow" tool. Pricing starts at $49/mo for the Basic plan (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="poweradspy" variant="hero" />

### 3. PiPiADS — The gold standard for TikTok-specific sweeps research

<ToolLogo slug="pipiads" size="lg" showName />

TikTok is currently a goldmine for high-energy, UGC-style sweepstakes ads. PiPiADS is the only tool on this list that treats TikTok as a first-class citizen. Their database is the deepest in the category, and their integration with the TikTok Creative Center makes it easy to spot trending sounds and formats.

However, it's very narrow. It's weak on Meta and offers zero landing page archiving. If you're a TikTok-first affiliate, it's a no-brainer. If you're a generalist, it's just one piece of the puzzle. Pricing starts at $77/mo for the Starter plan (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="pipiads" variant="hero" />

### 4. AdSpy — Unmatched historical data for spotting long-term sweeps trends

<ToolLogo slug="adspy" size="lg" showName />

AdSpy is the old guard. Its biggest strength is a 10-year historical database of Meta ads. If you want to see how sweepstakes angles have evolved over a decade or use complex Boolean search (AND/OR/NOT) to find very specific niches, AdSpy is the tool.

The trade-off is the cost and the lack of modern affiliate features. There is no landing page archiving and no network classification. You're paying for the archive. At $149/mo (as of April 2026), it's one of the more expensive options for a recurring subscription.

### 5. BigSpy — A low-cost entry point for multi-platform sampling

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

For beginners who aren't ready to drop hundreds of dollars, BigSpy is a solid starting point. It covers Meta, TikTok, and Pinterest, and it actually has a free tier that doesn't require a credit card.

The "free" experience is limited, and like PowerAdSpy, it lacks the depth of a specialist. You won't find landing page archives or network-specific filters here. It's a tool for sampling the market before you commit to a professional stack. Pricing ranges from Free to $249/mo for Enterprise (as of April 2026).

## How to pick
If you are running Meta-only sweeps and want to clone landing pages quickly, AdRecon is the logical choice. If you are diversifying into Native ads, PowerAdSpy is non-negotiable. For those attacking TikTok, PiPiADS is the only serious option. Most professional operators I know actually use a combination of two—usually a Meta specialist and a Native or TikTok tool.
