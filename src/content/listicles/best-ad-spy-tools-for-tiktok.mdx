## How we ranked these for TikTok ad research

TikTok is a distinct ecosystem with distinct research needs. A TikTok ad spy tool has to:

- Index the TikTok Ads Library with reasonable freshness
- Handle short-form video creative cleanly (hook analysis, first 3 seconds, captions, audio)
- Surface TikTok Shop product data where relevant
- Let you filter by region, vertical, advertiser, and creative format

Generalist tools — the ones that cover six or seven platforms — almost always fall short on at least two of those criteria. Specialists go deep on all four. That's why this ranking puts PiPiADS and Minea at the top and the generalists below.

Our weighting for this list:

- **TikTok Ads Library depth (35% weight)** — Raw index coverage.
- **Short-form creative handling (25% weight)** — Hook analysis, frame-by-frame, audio, captions.
- **TikTok Shop / product integration (20% weight)** — Product research for e-commerce angles.
- **Price value (20% weight)** — Monthly cost vs. depth.
