## A note on scope

AdRecon is a Meta-only affiliate-focused research tool, and it's not on this list because it's not built for e-commerce. Dropshipping and DTC e-commerce operators get more out of Minea, Foreplay, or PiPiADS — tools purpose-built for the product-first research e-commerce actually needs.

If you also run affiliate campaigns alongside your e-commerce brand (uncommon but not rare), AdRecon is the affiliate-side complement to Minea's e-commerce side. For a pure DTC operator, skip AdRecon and read on.

## How we ranked these for e-commerce

E-commerce research has specific needs:

- **Multi-platform coverage.** Most DTC brands run Meta + TikTok + Pinterest + (sometimes) Google Shopping or Display. One-platform specialists don't fit unless you're on that one platform.
- **Product-first indexing.** "Which product is winning" matters more than "which advertiser is winning."
- **Creative iteration support.** DTC brands test creatives weekly — the tool should support high-velocity research.
- **Influencer ad visibility.** Paid partnerships are a real channel for DTC; tools that hide creator ads miss signal.
- **Supplier / sourcing hints.** For dropshipping and newer DTC brands, supplier research is part of the workflow.

We weighted:

- **Multi-platform coverage (30% weight)** — Meta + TikTok + Pinterest minimum, extras for Google Shopping / Display.
- **Product + supplier research (25% weight)** — Product-level data and sourcing hints.
- **Creative workflow (20% weight)** — Save, organize, brief, share creative references.
- **Influencer ad coverage (15% weight)** — Creator ads indexed alongside brand ads.
- **Price value (10% weight)** — Total cost for the feature set.

Full methodology at [/methodology/](/methodology/).

## #1 — Minea

<ToolLogo slug="minea" size="lg" showName mode="mark" />
<Rating value={8.8} />

**Best for:** DTC brands and e-commerce operators across multiple platforms.
**Pricing:** From EUR 49/month Starter, EUR 99/month Premium, EUR 399/month Business (as of April 2026). Limited free surface.
**Standout feature:** Product + supplier + influencer research integrated with multi-platform ad data.

Minea is the e-commerce category leader. The product is built for this job — every feature and framing decision is tuned for DTC and dropshipping operators rather than affiliate marketers or generalists.

What Minea does particularly well for e-commerce:

- **Coverage matches where e-commerce actually runs.** Meta, TikTok, Pinterest — the three platforms DTC brands use most. Plus influencer ads on Instagram and TikTok.
- **Product-first indexing.** Ads are grouped by product, not just by advertiser. For someone researching "what fitness supplements are winning on TikTok right now," Minea returns a product-ranked view, not an advertiser-ranked view.
- **Supplier hints.** Minea surfaces likely AliExpress listings for tracked products — one-click from winning product to sourcing.
- **Shopify-adjacent tooling.** Store recognition, rough traffic estimation, Shopify theme identification.
- **Influencer ad integration.** Creator partnerships indexed as ads — critical for DTC brands running creator marketing as a real channel.

**Where Minea loses points:** Not affiliate-network-aware (irrelevant for e-commerce). EU-focused pricing and defaults. Higher-tier pricing (EUR 399 Business) escalates fast. Less collaborative than Foreplay for creative teams.

**Why #1 for e-commerce:** Purpose-built for the job. Everything else on this list is a general tool used for e-commerce, not an e-commerce tool.

## How to choose for e-commerce

Pick by platform mix and team:

- **Multi-platform DTC** → Minea. Default pick.
- **TikTok-first DTC** → PiPiADS.
- **DTC with in-house or agency creative team** → Foreplay + Minea.
- **Pre-revenue or validation** → BigSpy free + Meta Ad Library.
- **Multi-surface mature DTC (Google Shopping + Display + Meta + TikTok)** → PowerAdSpy.
- **Senior research lead studying Meta history** → AdSpy secondary.

Common e-commerce stacks:

- **Minea alone** — Standard DTC.
- **Minea + Foreplay** — Research + creative workflow. Agency-or-brand-team DTC.
- **PiPiADS + Minea** — TikTok depth plus multi-platform breadth.
- **Minea + Motion** — Research plus performance reporting.

## Our verdict

For DTC and e-commerce specifically, Minea wins. The multi-platform coverage, product-first framing, supplier hints, and influencer ad integration fit the job better than any generalist on this list.

Foreplay is the creative-workflow partner for brand teams and agencies shipping DTC creative. PiPiADS is the TikTok specialist. BigSpy is the validation tier. PowerAdSpy and AdSpy fit narrower sub-personas.

AdRecon doesn't make this list for a reason. It's built for affiliate marketers, not DTC. Don't buy the wrong tool for your job. If you're DTC, Minea is almost always the right answer.
