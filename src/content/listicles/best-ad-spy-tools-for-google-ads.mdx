## TL;DR
For comprehensive Google Search and Display coverage, PowerAdSpy is the top pick. If you are on a tight budget, BigSpy is the best entry point. Note that AdRecon does not cover Google, but remains the best choice for Meta-specific affiliate intelligence.

## Why this list exists
Google Ads is a different beast than Meta or TikTok. Between the intent-based nature of Search and the massive reach of the Display Network (GDN), finding "winning" creatives requires a tool that can actually index these surfaces. Most "ad spies" claim to do everything but are shallow on Google. This list separates the generalists from the tools that actually provide actionable Google data.

## How we ranked
We didn't rely on marketing brochures. Our team tested these tools hands-on, scoring them across eight weighted dimensions: platform coverage, data freshness, price, UX, landing page archiving, offer identification, affiliate-specific focus, and search capability.

## The ranking

### 1. PowerAdSpy — The most comprehensive option for Google Search and Display coverage

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

When you need to see what is actually running on Google, PowerAdSpy is the most reliable choice in the current market. Unlike many competitors that only scrape a few banners, PowerAdSpy provides a wider window into both the Search and Display sides of the house.

It is particularly useful for media buyers who don't just stick to one channel. If you are running a campaign that spans Google Search, YouTube, and Native networks like Taboola or Outbrain, this tool keeps your research in one place.

The downside is the UX; it feels dated and can be clunky to navigate. However, for raw data access, it wins. Pricing starts at a Basic plan of $49/mo (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="poweradspy" variant="hero" />

### 2. BigSpy — A solid, low-cost entry point for Google Display research

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

BigSpy is the "sampler" tool. It is one of the few platforms that offers a legitimate free tier without requiring a credit card upfront. This makes it the ideal starting point for beginners who want to see if Google Display is a viable channel for their offer before committing a monthly budget.

While it covers Google, it is shallower than PowerAdSpy. You won't find every single niche ad, but you'll find enough to get a pulse on the market. It lacks deep affiliate-network classification, meaning you have to do the manual work of figuring out if an ad is for a ClickBank or MaxWeb offer.

Pricing is highly accessible, with a Basic plan at $9/mo (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="bigspy" variant="hero" />

### 3. AdRecon — Zero Google coverage, but the gold standard for Meta affiliate intelligence

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

To be completely honest: AdRecon is not a Google spy tool. We do not cover Google, YouTube, or Search. If your only goal is to spy on Google Ads, AdRecon is not the tool for you.

However, we include it here because most Google affiliates eventually move their winners over to Meta to scale. While PowerAdSpy and BigSpy are "jack of all trades," AdRecon is a specialist. We provide affiliate-network classification (ClickBank, Digistore24, etc.) and a Landing Page Ripper that downloads the full HTML/CSS of a page—features that the Google-focused tools simply don't have.

If you've found a winner on Google and want to see how the top Meta affiliates are positioning that same offer, this is the stack to use. It is priced at $299 / $599 lifetime (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="adrecon" variant="hero" />

### 4. Minea — Better for e-commerce than Google Search affiliates

<ToolLogo slug="minea" size="lg" showName />

Minea is a powerhouse for dropshippers, and while it does have Google coverage, it is tuned for product-based ads. If you are looking for "winning products" to sell via Shopify, Minea's workflow is superior to almost anything else on this list.

For the CPA affiliate running lead-gen or health offers on Google Search, Minea is less useful. It lacks the granular search-intent data you need for high-ticket affiliate marketing.

Pricing starts at a Starter plan of €49/mo (as of April 2026).

### 5. PiPiADS — TikTok specialist with minimal utility for Google Ads

<ToolLogo slug="pipiads" size="lg" showName />

PiPiADS is the undisputed king of TikTok. If your strategy involves TikTok-first creatives, you need this tool. However, its Google coverage is an afterthought.

Using PiPiADS for Google Ads is like using a hammer to turn a screw—it might technically work in some cases, but it's the wrong tool for the job. We've ranked it last here because its value proposition is almost entirely centered on TikTok.

Pricing starts at $77/mo (as of April 2026).

## How to pick
If your priority is raw Google Search and Display data, go with PowerAdSpy. If you are just testing the waters and want a free or cheap option, BigSpy is the move. If you are a professional affiliate who primarily uses Meta but wants the best possible intelligence for ClickBank or BuyGoods offers, AdRecon is the only choice, despite the lack of Google coverage.
