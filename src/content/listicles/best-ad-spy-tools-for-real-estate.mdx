## TL;DR
For most real estate and mortgage affiliates, AdRecon is the top choice due to its ability to rip landing pages and track proven Meta creatives. If you are scaling into Native ads (Taboola/Outbrain), go with PowerAdSpy. For deep historical archives, AdSpy remains the industry standard.

## Why this list exists
Real estate lead gen is a game of "the funnel." Finding a winning ad creative is easy, but figuring out the exact survey flow or lead-capture page that actually converts a homeowner is the hard part. Most spy tools show you the ad and then send you to a live link that might be dead or changed. You need a tool that lets you see the full path from click to lead.

## How we ranked
We evaluated these tools based on eight specific dimensions: platform coverage, data freshness, pricing structure, user experience (UX), landing page archiving capabilities, offer classification, affiliate-specific focus, and search granularity. 

## The ranking

### 1. AdRecon — The best for ripping high-converting lead-gen funnels

<ToolLogo slug="adrecon" size="lg" showName />

In real estate lead gen, the "magic" happens on the landing page. AdRecon wins here because of the Landing Page Ripper. Instead of just seeing a screenshot, you can download the full HTML/CSS and assets as a ZIP. This allows you to analyze the exact psychology of a competitor's survey or lead form without guessing.

Since We also don't cover TikTok or Google. But for the Meta-focused affiliate, the ability to group winning offers and see "days-running" signals is a massive shortcut to profitability.

Pricing is a one-time payment of $299 or $599 for lifetime access (as of April 2026), which is a relief for operators who hate monthly subscriptions.

<Screenshot slug="adrecon" variant="hero" />

### 2. PowerAdSpy — Unbeatable for those scaling into Native ads

<ToolLogo slug="poweradspy" size="lg" showName />

If your real estate strategy involves moving beyond Meta into Native advertising—think Taboola, Outbrain, or RevContent—PowerAdSpy is the only logical choice. Native ads often work better for "advertorial" style real estate offers (e.g., "5 Reasons Homeowners in Florida are Switching Lenders"), and PowerAdSpy has the broadest coverage for these surfaces.

The downside is that the UX feels a bit dated, and it lacks the specialized affiliate-network classification found in more niche tools. It's a broad hammer, not a scalpel.

Plans range from a Basic $49/mo to a Premium $249/mo (as of April 2026).

<Screenshot slug="poweradspy" variant="hero" />

### 3. AdSpy — The gold standard for historical Meta research

<ToolLogo slug="adspy" size="lg" showName />

AdSpy is the veteran in the room. If you need to know what worked for mortgage leads in 2018 to spot a recurring trend, AdSpy's 10-year archive is unmatched. Their Boolean search (AND/OR/NOT) is far more powerful than the basic filters found in most other tools, allowing you to drill down into very specific niches.

However, AdSpy is missing a critical piece of the lead-gen puzzle: landing page archives. You can see the ad, but you can't "rip" the funnel. Additionally, the $149/mo recurring cost (as of April 2026) can be a steep monthly tax on your margins.

<Screenshot slug="adspy" variant="hero" />

### 4. BigSpy — The lowest barrier to entry for beginners

<ToolLogo slug="bigspy" size="lg" showName />

For someone just starting in real estate lead gen who isn't sure if they want to run on Meta, Pinterest, or YouTube, BigSpy is a great sampling tool. They offer a legitimate free tier (as of April 2026) that doesn't require a credit card, which is rare in this category.

Pricing ranges from Free to $249/mo for VIP Enterprise (as of April 2026).

### 5. Foreplay — Best for organizing creative briefs and mood boards

<ToolLogo slug="foreplay" size="lg" showName />

Foreplay isn't a "spy tool" in the traditional sense of hunting for offers; it's a workflow tool. If you run a real estate agency and need to present "creative directions" to a client or a designer, Foreplay's mood boards and swipe files are the best in the business.

The UX is modern and slick, but it's built for agencies, not solo affiliates. It doesn't have landing page archiving or affiliate-network classification. If you need to find a winning mortgage offer and clone the funnel, this isn't the tool for you.

Pricing starts at $49/mo for the Inspiration plan (as of April 2026).

## How to pick
If you are a solo affiliate running Meta ads for mortgage or real estate offers, prioritize landing page archiving—AdRecon is your best bet. If you are a media buyer diversifying into Native traffic, PowerAdSpy is the requirement. For those who need a massive historical archive and advanced search logic, stick with AdSpy.
